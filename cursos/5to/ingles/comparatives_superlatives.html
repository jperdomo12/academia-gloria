Comparatives & Superlatives 🏆

Aprende a comparar dos cosas y a decir cuál es “el más” o “la más” en inglés.

Empezar Evaluación
🏆

Idea clave

Compare & choose

Comparative

Two things: A is taller than B.

Superlative

One is the top: the tallest.

Long adjectives

more beautiful than / the most beautiful.

➕

-ER

Long → longer. Fast → faster.

👑

THE -EST

Long → the longest. Fast → the fastest.

🌟

MORE / MOST

more beautiful than / the most beautiful.

⚠️

Irregular

good → better → the best. bad → worse → the worst.

Comparative: comparar 2 cosas

A + is + comparative + than + B

Trees are taller than people.

tall → taller than

Science is more interesting than History.

interesting → more interesting than

Superlative: “el/la más”

the + superlative

Cheetahs are the fastest animals.

fast → the fastest

T-Rex is the most dangerous dinosaur.

dangerous → the most dangerous

🖼️

Ejemplos visuales

🥇 🪣

Gold is more expensive than plastic.

Plastic is cheaper than gold.

✈️ 🚢

A plane is faster than a ship.

A ship is slower than a plane.

🦄 👹

A unicorn is prettier than a troll.

A troll is uglier than a unicorn.

🥁 🎼

The drums are louder than a harp.

A harp is quieter than the drums.

+ ER

long → longer
slow → slower
tall → taller

THE + EST

long → the longest
slow → the slowest
fast → the fastest

MORE

beautiful → more beautiful
expensive → more expensive

THE MOST

dangerous → the most dangerous
interesting → the most interesting

Big / Hot / Fat

big → bigger → the biggest
hot → hotter → the hottest
fat → fatter → the fattest

Happy / Pretty

happy → happier → the happiest
pretty → prettier → the prettiest

Good / Bad

good → better → the best
bad → worse → the worst
