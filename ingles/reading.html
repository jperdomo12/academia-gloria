📖 Reading Challenges

Text 1: My Name is Lucy

"Hello! My name is Lucy. I am eight years old. I live in a small town with my family. I have one brother and one sister. My favourite colour is blue. Every day, I go to school in the morning. I like English and Art, but I don't like Maths. After school, I play with my friends or read a book."

1. How old is Lucy?

2. What is her favorite color?

Text 2: Sam and the Farm

"Sam is at the farm today. It is a sunny day. He sees five white sheep and a big brown horse. The horse's name is Barnaby. Sam gives an apple to the horse. Sam is very happy."

1. What color is the horse?
