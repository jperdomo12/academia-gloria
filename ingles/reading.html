Text 1

My Name is Lucy

"Hello! My name is Lucy. I am eight years old. I live in a small town with my family. I have one brother and one sister. My favourite colour is blue.

Every day, I go to school in the morning. I like English and Art, but I don't like Maths. After school, I play with my friends or read a book. In the evening, I have dinner with my family and watch TV. I go to bed at nine o'clock."

1. What is the girl's name?

2. How old is she?

3. Where does Lucy live?

4. What is her favourite colour?

5. What subjects does she like?

6. What does she do after school?

7. When does she have dinner?

8. What time does she go to bed?

Text 2

Sam and the Farm

"Sam is ten years old. Today, he is visiting his grandparents at the farm. It is a very hot and sunny day. Sam is wearing a yellow t-shirt and his favourite red cap. He is very excited to see the animals.

In the barn, he sees five white sheep and a big brown horse called Barnaby. Sam gives a big red apple to the horse. Barnaby is very hungry! Later, Sam helps his grandfather to find some eggs in the garden."

1. How old is Sam?

2. Where is Sam today?

3. What is the weather like?

4. What is Sam wearing on his head?

5. How many sheep are there?

6. What is the horse's name?

7. What does Sam give to the horse?

8. What does he find in the garden?
