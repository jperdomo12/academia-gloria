Idea clave

Adjective = describe

What is it?

An adjective describes a noun. (Un adjetivo describe a un sustantivo.)

Where does it go?

Usually before the noun (Generalmente antes del sustantivo): a blue ball.

What can it describe?

Colour, size, shape, texture, weather...

What is an adjective?

An adjective is a word that describes a noun: a person, a place, a thing or an idea. (Un adjetivo es una palabra que describe a un sustantivo: una persona, un lugar, una cosa o una idea.)

adjective + noun

A blue ball

blue describes ball.

A small eye

small describes eye.

Soft texture

soft describes texture.

A fast horse

fast describes horse.

Golden rule ✨

“In English, the adjective usually goes BEFORE the noun. (En inglés, el adjetivo generalmente va ANTES del sustantivo.)”

Correct ✅

She has blue eyes.

Not usually ❌

She has eyes blue.
