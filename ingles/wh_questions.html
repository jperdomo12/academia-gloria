Aprende a preguntar en inglés usando what, who, where, when, why, how y sus formas especiales.

Idea clave

Cada WH pregunta algo

WHAT

Pregunta por cosas o acciones.

WHERE / WHEN

Lugar y tiempo.

HOW

Pregunta cómo, edad, cantidad o frecuencia.

🧩

WH-word

What, who, where, when, why, how...

⚙️

Auxiliary

Do, does, is, are. Ayudan a construir la pregunta.

👤

Subject

You, she, he, they, your sister...

✅

Answer

La respuesta te da la pista.

🗺️

Mapa rápido de preguntas

WHAT

Cosas / acciones

What are you doing? → I am reading.

WHO

Personas

Who is she? → She is my sister.

WHERE

Lugar

Where do you live? → In Paris.

WHEN

Tiempo / fecha

When is your birthday? → June 5th.

WHY

Razón

Why are you sad? → Because I feel sick.

HOW

Cómo / estado

How are you? → I'm fine.

Fórmula fácil

WH-word + auxiliary + subject + verb...?

With DO

Where do you live?

With IS

Who is she?

With ARE

How are you?

With DOES

What time does he get up?

Truco de Gloria ✨

“Miro la respuesta y busco qué información falta.”

Answer: At 8:00.

Pregunta por hora → What time

Answer: Because I am happy.

Pregunta por razón → Why

WHAT

What is this? → This is a red ball.

WHO

Who is that girl? → She is my sister.

WHERE

Where are they from? → They are from Canada.

WHEN

When is your birthday? → It is on November 9.

WHY

Why are you sad? → Because I feel sick.

HOW

How are you? → I am fine, thanks.

HOW OLD

How old are you? → I am ten.

HOW MANY

How many pens do you have? → 3 pens.

HOW MUCH

How much is it? → 30 dollars.

HOW OFTEN

How often do you study? → Five times a week.

DO con I / you / we / they

Where do you live?

DOES con he / she / it

What time does he go to bed?

IS con he / she / it

Who is she?

ARE con you / they

How old are you?

Mini práctica

Escribe la WH-word correcta. Después pulsa “Check”.

_____ are you from? → I am from Canada.

_____ is your birthday? → It is on November 9.

_____ are you sad? → Because I feel sick.

_____ is that girl? → She is my sister.

_____ pens do you have? → 3 pens.

Construye preguntas

Mira la respuesta y piensa qué pregunta usarías.

Answer: Running.

Question: What are you doing?

Answer: With Paul.

Question: Who do you play with?

Answer: Three times a week.

Question: How often do you study?

Answer: Because I feel good.

Question: Why are you happy?

Tip

Si la respuesta empieza con “because”, casi siempre la pregunta empieza con why.
